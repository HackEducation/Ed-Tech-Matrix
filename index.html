The Ed-Tech (Industry) Matrix


Who’s investing in education technology? What are the relationships between investors and startups? Between politics and policies? What does the “map” of the ed-tech industry look like? What are the relationships between companies, entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions?


I would like to build better “maps” of the ed-tech industry in the hopes that we we can more easily navigate it. I’d like to create an open database of education startup funding, for starters, and host it on GitHub so that people can contribute and fork.


So clearly, this section of Hack Education is under development. In the meantime:

And yes, I’m calling it a “matrix” and not an “ecosystem.” The latter – a popular term in the tech realm, I realize – connotes a natural, symbiotic, and ideally sustainable system. I’m not sure that’s what we have here in education... And that's partially why I think we should make maps so we can better tell where the heck we might be headed.
