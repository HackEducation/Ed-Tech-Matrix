Who's investing in education technology? What are the relationships between investors and startups? Between politics and policies? Between dollars and trends? Who's funding teacher-founded startups? Who's funding women and minority-led startups? What does the "map" of the ed-tech industry look like? What are the relationships between companies, entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions?

I would like to build better "maps" of the ed-tech industry in the hopes that we we can more easily navigate it. To that end, I have started work on an open database of education startup funding. This database (like this site) is hosted on GitHub so that people can contribute and fork.

Via the GitHub interface, you can ask questions that will help shape the research (and shape the data I collect). To do so, submit an “issue.”

This project is under development. You can find updates here. In the meantime, you can read some of the other posts I’ve written on Hack Education about startup funding:
